Commenting on a card

Leave comments on cards to communicate with other members of your board.

Leave comments on cards to communicate with other members of your board. This can be used to update the status of a project piece, discuss option possibilities between members, and more.

To comment on a card, perform the following actions:

  1. Click on the card for which you would like to leave a comment.
  2. In the Activity area, in the Write a comment... field, enter your comment.
    1. To receive notifications when the card is updated, check the Watch checkbox.
    2. To upload a file as part of your comment, click the Add an attachment icon.
    3. To tag another member of the board, click the Mention a member icon.
    4. To use emojis in your comment, click the Add emoji icon.
    5. To insert a link to another card or Trello board, click the Add card icon.
  3. Click Save. This will add the comment to the top of the activity list.
