Creating a board

Create a board in Trello to start organizing the way you work.

Trello boards are how you organize your projects. Creating a new board allows you to start a new project and get to work organizing what needs to do be done for it.

To create a board, perform the following actions:

  1. In the top right corner, click
  2. In the Create window, click .
  3. In the pop-up window, in the field, enter a name for your board.
  4. If you would like to add your board to one of your teams, click the drop down box to select which team you would like your board to be part of. If you aren't part of any teams or don't want to share your board with a team, click No team.
  5. In the drop down box, select your privacy settings for the board.
  6. On the right side of the pop up window, choose a background image for your board.
    Note: This is purely for cosmetic purposes only and can be changed later.
  7. If you would like to create your board using a template, click the button. This will show you the many templates to choose from in a variety of areas, including business, education, engineering, and others.
  8. When you're done setting up your board, click the button to create your board.
