Adding a sticker

Use stickers to add an extra touch to your cards.

To add a sticker to a card, perform the following actions:

  1. In the top right corner of your board, click Show Menu.
  2. In the Menu panel, click Stickers.
  3. Click and drag a sticker to a card to place it.
  4. To remove a sticker, hover over the sticker and click Remove.
