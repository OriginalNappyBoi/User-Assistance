Creating a business team

Create a business team in Trello Business Class to have more security, administrative controls, and unlimited Power-Ups.

Note: Creating a business team required Trello Business Class, a premium version of Trello. This feature is not available for users of the free version of Trello.

  4. In the field, enter a description for your team.

  6. In the Invite Your Team window, in the field, enter your teammates' emails.
  7. In the field, edit the existing message invitation message or type your own message.
  8. When you're done setting up your board, click the button to invite people to your team.
