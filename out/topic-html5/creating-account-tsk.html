creating an account

Step by step directions on how to create a trello account.

  1. Open a web browser of your choosing.
  2. Next type into your search engine Trello and click on the website Trello.com.
  3. Locate the sign up option and click on it.
  4. You can choose to enter an email of your choosing or continue using Google.
  5. Now fill in the information, enter your full and enter in a valid password.
  6. Click sign up and it will ask you to verify your account with the email you provided, once you do that you are done.
